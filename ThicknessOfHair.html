How to Get a Thick and Dense Hair ?

Today I am going to talk about common hair problems that can happen because of some health issues, styling damages, but don't worry. There are ways to increase the volume and density of your hair.

1. Onions

Have you heard of using onions for hair care? You can take onions and make juice out of it. You can then massage onion juice on your scalp or you can make a hair mask by blending one onion and one tablespoon of honey. Leave it as it is for at least thirty minutes and then wash it off. Onions are packed with sulphur. This is an important mineral that helps to boost collagen which is essential for the hair's thickness, strength and structure.

2. Fenugreek Seeds

Next, did you know that fenugreek seed or "methi" is great for your hair growth. Fenugreek seeds are packed with nutrients like protein, iron and potassium. These all work together to give your hair the needed growth, strength, health and volume. Apply Fenugreek seed on your hair by making a hair mask with ground Fenugreek seeds and coconut oil.

3. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is one of nature's best gifts for hair. Aloe vera is great for hair density, because it contains enzymes that help to promote hair growth. Use aloe vera for your hair. Simply extract the gel from the Aloe Vera Leaf and apply onto your scalp and hair. Leave it on for thirty minutes and then wash your hair as usual.

4. Amla or Indian Gooseberry

Amla also known as Indian Gooseberry is super for your hair thickness and volume. Amla has high vitamin C content, which is essential for the production of collagen. Collagen is a protein that gives hair its strength and structure. To use amla for your hair. You can make a hair mask by adding two tablespoons of amla powder with three tablespoons of water or coconut oil to form a paste. Apply the paste to your hair and scalp and leave it for an hour before washing it off.

5. Henna

Henna also has a natural conditioning effect on hair. It helps to add shine and smoothness while reducing frizz. This can make your hair look and feel healthier. To use henna for your hair. You can make a henna paste. Inorder to make henna paste, add henna powder with one and a half cups of water, and a tablespoon of lemon juice. Apply this paste to your hair and scalp and leave it on for two to four hours before washing it off.

6. Oil Massage

Another important routine of hair care is oil massage. Massage your scalp with oil regularly. The oil can be anyone like coconut oil, olive oil or castor oil. This will stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.

7. Diet

The last but certainly not the least is your diet. You must have a diet which is rich in protein, iron, vitamin C, Omega 3 fatty acids and B vitamins. Good food sources of all these nutrients include leafy greens, oranges, strawberries, ball peppers, kiwis and chia seeds.

Increase the volume and density of hair by all these different methods and see which is best for you and your hair by yourself, how it is so good for your hair for growth, for the density and overall for your general health. So take the chance to do something for yourself.
