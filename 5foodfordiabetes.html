5 Best food for Diabetes control

Bingeing on unhealthy, sugary and processed food has immensely contributed to increasing this problem. Studies reveal that by 2040, 700 million people worldwide will have diabetes. Taking preventive and corrective measures as soon as possible is very important today. I am going to share a magical drink for people suffering from diabetes to help them to keep that blood sugar level in check and lead a very healthy and peaceful life.


1. Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

Amla or Indian Gooseberry is an amazing trick that would help people suffering from high blood sugar levels and insulin problems. Amla is a superb fruit that would work wonders for your body and helps to prevent pancreatitis. It keeps our blood sugar checked.

Diabetes has grown from 108 million in 1982 to 422 million in 2014. Diabetes causes problems like kidney failure, heart issues. Controlling the blood sugar level is the primary task of diabetic patients.

Food control is the major factor in controlling diabetes. Some excellent food which one must consume to manage diabetes number one green leafy vegetables. Green leafy vegetables are low in calories. They are rich in fibre. One must consume spinach, Methi leaves and drumstick leaves in their regular meals.

Among all green leafy vegetables, drumstick leaves really reduce diabetes. They are rich in ascorbic acid which leads to increased insulin secretion in our body.


2. Whole Grains

Whole grains can really help reduce the latter. Whole grains have a lesser glycemic index. Whole grains like brown rice, ragi and rai are excellent foods to control blood sugar.

Ragi is an excellent food among all millets. It reduces oxidative stress and inflammation, which in turn helps control diabetes.



3. Berries

Berries like strawberries, blueberries , raspberries and blackberries are especially excellent food. They have antioxidants that reduce oxidative stress which is very common in diabetic patients. Berries are very good in vitamin C too which are known to keep blood sugar levels in balance.


4. Beans

Beans are low in glycemic index and so it effectively reduces blood sugar level. GI-The glycemic index of soya beans is 15 while kidney beans are 28 and chickpeas 33. These are complex carbohydrates. So the body digests them slower than any other carbohydrate diets.

Eating beans would really help even with weight loss. So and so blood sugar and cholesterol is reduced.


5. Citric Fruits

Adding citric fruits to your daily diet. Oranges and lemon have amazing anti-diabetic effects. Modern research believes that they have bioflavonoids. All this is available in orange and lemon and this has anti-diabetic effect. All these citrus fruits are excellent in vitamin C. All these citrus fruits help in controlling blood sugar levels. Green leafy vegetables, whole grain, berries, beans and citrus fruits. All these should be the major part of a diabetic person's diet.

Moreover, you should also control yourself from consuming food, which can disturb your sugar level and avoid food like white rice, white bread, pumpkins, white potatoes, refined sugars. Sugary drinks, sweet cakes, pastries and packaged snacks and packaged foods, avoid consuming all these foods which can spike your blood sugar rapidly.

More importantly, keep eating every two hours. The Major mistake many make is by skipping breakfast or having a very late breakfast. Make sure you eat something within one hour after you wake up. You can have warm lemon juice with Chia seeds in the morning or amla powder with a pinch of turmeric in warm water. These morning drinks can help to maintain your blood sugar level throughout the day.
